Privacy Policy & Data Protection

Data Protection Commitment

IndiaAI is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the secure handling of your personal information in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Residential Address
  • Age
Biometric Data
  • Facial Photograph
  • Facial Features (AI Analysis)
  • Encrypted Biometric Hash

AI Processing & Deduplication

How We Use AI Technology

Your photograph is processed using advanced facial recognition algorithms to create a unique biometric template. This template is used solely for duplicate application detection and is immediately encrypted for security.

Data Encryption

All biometric data is encrypted using industry-standard algorithms

Secure Storage

Data stored in secure servers with restricted access

Limited Retention

Data retained only for the duration required by law

Purpose of Data Collection

  • Duplicate application detection and prevention
  • Identity verification and authentication
  • Ensuring fair and transparent selection processes
  • Maintaining examination integrity
  • Not Used For: Surveillance, profiling, or commercial purposes

Your Rights

Data Access & Correction

You have the right to access your personal data and request corrections if inaccurate information is found.

Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw your consent for data processing, though this may affect your application eligibility.

Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your data protection rights, please contact: privacy@indiaai.gov.in
