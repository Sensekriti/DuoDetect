About the IndiaAI Face Authentication Challenge

The IndiaAI Face Authentication Challenge aims to strengthen the integrity of public examination processes through advanced AI-powered duplicate application detection.

Step-by-Step Application Process

1
Prepare Your Documents

Ensure you have a valid passport-size photograph ready for upload. The photo should be clear, recent, and against a light background.

2
Fill Application Form

Complete all required fields with accurate information. Double-check your contact details and personal information.

3
Upload Photograph

Upload your passport-size photo. The AI system will analyze the image for quality and perform initial verification.

4
AI Analysis & Verification

Our advanced AI system performs facial recognition analysis to detect potential duplicate applications across the database.

Photograph Requirements

  • Recent passport-size photograph (taken within last 6 months)
  • Clear, front-facing view with both eyes visible
  • Plain light-colored background
  • No hats, sunglasses, or face coverings
  • File formats: JPG, PNG, JPEG (Max: 2MB)

How It Works

Our advanced facial recognition system uses one-to-many matching to compare new applications against existing records. This ensures each candidate can submit only one valid application, maintaining the integrity of the selection process.
